My Portfolio

A splash of my work starting with motion graphics.

Kian's Fish Intro

My client, Kian, requested an overlay and non-overlay version of an animated fishing intro. I like the outcome of this one.

Deveral's Intro

E-Sports team, Deveral, ordered me to make a simple intro for their videos. They were very specific but we both loved the outcome of this!

Animated Switch

A motion piece I created when I was bored; the box contents of the stock Nintendo Switch. Not sponsored. What a nice console!



FreezLynx's Intro

Some fan motion-graphics for FreezLynx. I inspired this from his original graphics and added my own ideas to that inspiration.

TGW Graphic

Motion Design group, The Groovy Warehouse, invited me to join them and I made this video to announce that I had joined them.

#OPFXC1

#OPFXC1 was a motion design contest hosted by OblivionFX and Prepy2D. I put a decent amount of effort in and placed 1st!
